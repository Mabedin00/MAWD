How to Play Rick and Morty Trivia!

What is this game?

Rick and Morty Trivia is one of the three games where you can bet your MAWDollars against the computer. You are given three images of characters from the popular adult swim show Rick and Morty. You are given four choices per question and must answer all questions by choosing one out of the 4 options. You will either lose, break even, or gain MAWDollars depending on the number of answers you get correct.

How do I determine how much I win or lose?

If you get
  • 0 questions correct: You lose the money you bet.
  • 1 question correct: You lose half the money you bet
  • 2 questions correct: You break even.
  • 3 questions correct: You double up.
