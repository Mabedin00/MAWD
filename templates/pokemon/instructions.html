How to play Pokemon Versus!

What is this game?

Pokemon Versus is one of the three games where you can bet your MAWDollars against the computer. You pick a pokemon and the computer randomly picks one (out of four). The pokemon then fight and the winner is determined by type advantage

How do I know type advantage?

How do I know which pokemon wins?

This is best explained through an example. For example, let's say:
You bet 10 MAWDollars and chose Paras, which is "bug" and "grass"
The computer chose Krabby, which is "water". Because Krabby only has one type, its secondary type will the primary type as well, which is "water"

Each pokemon will then use its primary type to attack the other pokemon's primary and secondary type. In this case:
Krabby attacks Paras. Water is 1x to bug and 0.5x to grass. The total damage is the sum, which is 1.5
Paras attacks Krabby. Bug is 1x to water. The total damage is the sum, which is 2
Because the difference is 0.5, with you choosing the pokemon doing more damage, you won!
The difference wasn't that great, so you only won 1.5x the amount you bet, which is 15.

How do I determine how much I win or lose?

If the difference is
  • 0 (tie): The amount you bet is returned.
  • Between 0.5 and 1.5 inclusive (won): 1.5x the amount you bet is given to you.
  • Between -0.5 and -1.5 inclusive (lost): 0.5 the amount you bet is given back.
  • Greater than 1.5 (won): 2x the amount you bet is given to you.
  • Less than 1.5 (lost): Lost what you bet.
