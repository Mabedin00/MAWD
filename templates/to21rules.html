Rules of To21

To21 starts with players making wagers

The dealer deals 2 cards to the players and two to himself (1 card face up, the other face down)

All cards count their face value in blackjack. Picture cards count as 10 and the ace can count as either 1 or 11.

Card suits have no meaning in To21. The total of any hand is the sum of the card values in the hand

Players must decide whether to stand or hit. By hitting, the player draws another card. By standing, the player chooses to keep their hand as is

The dealer acts last

If there is a tie, the player simply keeps their wager
